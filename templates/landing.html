TSMS Journal

The Social and Management Scientist (TSMS) is a peer reviewed Journal of Faculty of The Social Science, Adekunle Ajasin University, Akungba-Akoko, Ondo State, Nigeria.

It is an interdisciplinary journal that focuses on social, management and behavioural sciences.

The Journal creates an intellectual platform for presentation of innovative research ideas and critical research discussions. It is published in February and August every year. Research papers that are relevant in the field of social, economic, financial and political development are welcomed.
